## Extracted Text

> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of kill:: Determine the locale that should be used to affect the format and contents of diagnostic messages written to standard error.

## Context

**Behavior:** `LC_MESSAGES` determines the locale used for the format and contents of diagnostic messages written to stderr by the `kill` built-in. This affects error messages such as "No such process" or "Invalid signal name".
