## Extracted Text

> The grammar and lexical conventions in this section shall together describe the syntax for the locale definition source. The general conventions for this style of grammar are described in XCU 1.3 Grammar Conventions. The grammar shall take precedence over the text in this chapter.

## Context

The grammar and lexical conventions in XBD 7.4 together describe the syntax for locale definition source files. The general conventions are described in XCU 1.3 Grammar Conventions. Where the grammar and the descriptive text in Chapter 7 conflict, the grammar takes precedence.

**Shell relevance:** The shell does not parse locale definition files, but this grammar governs the locale data the shell consumes. See XBD 7.4.
