## Extracted Text

> The standard error shall be used only for diagnostic messages.

## Context

**Behavior:** The `command` built-in must use stderr exclusively for diagnostic (error) messages. No informational or status output may be written to stderr. The `-v` and `-V` outputs go to stdout, not stderr.

**Spec reference:** POSIX.1-2024, `utilities/command.html`, section STDERR (tag_20_22_11), line 182.
