## Extracted Text

> The <period>, <left-square-bracket>, and <backslash> shall be special except when used in a bracket expression (see 9.3.5 RE Bracket Expression). An expression containing a '[' that is unescaped and is not part of a bracket expression produces undefined results.

## Context

**Behavior:** Duplicate of SHALL-09-03-03-001: The period (`.`), left-square-bracket (`[`), and backslash (`\`) shall be special in BRE except inside bracket expressions. An unescaped `[` not part of a bracket expression produces undefined results.

**Shell relevance:** Same as SHALL-09-03-03-001. In shell patterns, `[` starts a bracket expression; an unmatched `[` produces undefined results.

**Spec reference:** Section 9.3.3, lines 153-154 in `basedefs/V1_chap09.html`.
