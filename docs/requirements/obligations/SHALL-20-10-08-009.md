## Extracted Text

> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of bg:: Determine the locale that should be used to affect the format and contents of diagnostic messages written to standard error.

## Context

`LC_MESSAGES` **shall** determine the locale that affects the format and contents of diagnostic messages written to stderr by `bg`. Error messages about invalid jobs, job control being disabled, etc., should be formatted per the `LC_MESSAGES` locale. Reference: bg utility ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES.
