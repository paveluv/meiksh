## Extracted Text

> The shell shall disable pathname expansion.

## Context

**Behavior:** The `-f` (`noglob`) option: when enabled, the shell must **disable pathname expansion** (globbing). Pattern characters (`*`, `?`, `[`) in words are treated as literal characters and not expanded against filenames.

**Applies to:** All shell modes.
