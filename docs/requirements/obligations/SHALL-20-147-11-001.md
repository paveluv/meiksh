## Extracted Text

> The standard error shall be used only for diagnostic messages.

## Context

**Behavior:** The `wait` utility shall write to stderr only for diagnostic messages. No regular output goes to stderr. The `wait` utility produces no stdout output at all.

**Conditions:** Diagnostic messages may be written when invalid operands are given (e.g., non-numeric, non-job-ID strings).
