## Extracted Text

> In a locale definition, the following mandatory keywords shall be recognized:: abmon

## Context

The `abmon` keyword shall be recognized in an LC_TIME locale definition. It introduces the definition of abbreviated month names.

**Shell relevance:** Abbreviated month names affect `%b` in `date`. See XBD 7.3.5.1.
