## Extracted Text

> If the currently executing AND-OR list within the list comprising the foreground job consists of a single pipeline in which all of the commands are simple commands, the shell shall either create a suspended job consisting of at least that AND-OR list and the remaining (if any) AND-OR lists in the same list, or create a suspended job consisting of just that AND-OR list and discard the remaining (if any) AND-OR lists in the same list.

## Context

**Foreground job suspension — simple pipeline case (detail):** When stopped by a catchable signal (SIGTSTP, SIGTTIN, SIGTTOU) and the AND-OR list is a single pipeline of simple commands only, create a suspended job containing at least that AND-OR list. The implementation may optionally include remaining AND-OR lists from the same list (allowing them to resume via `fg`), or may discard them. Either approach is conformant.

The suspended job is added to the job table, assigned a job number, and the shell reclaims the terminal (see SHALL-19-11-020, SHALL-19-11-027).
