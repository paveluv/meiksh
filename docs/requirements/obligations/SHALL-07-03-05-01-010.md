## Extracted Text

> In a locale definition, the following mandatory keywords shall be recognized:: ab_alt_mon

## Context

The `ab_alt_mon` keyword shall be recognized in an LC_TIME locale definition. It introduces the definition of alternative (nominative) abbreviated month names.

**Shell relevance:** Alternative abbreviated month names affect `%Ob` in `date`. See XBD 7.3.5.1.
