## Extracted Text

> The following tokens shall be processed (in addition to those string constants shown in the grammar):: A string of alphanumeric characters from the portable character set, the first of which is not a digit, consisting of at least one and at most {CHARCLASS_NAME_MAX} bytes, and optionally surrounded by double-quotes.

## Context

CHARCLASS is defined as a string of alphanumeric characters from the portable character set, the first of which is not a digit, consisting of at least 1 and at most {CHARCLASS_NAME_MAX} bytes, and optionally surrounded by double-quotes.

**Shell relevance:** The shell must accept character class names like `alpha`, `digit`, etc. in `[:name:]` bracket expressions within glob patterns. See XBD 7.4.1.
