## Extracted Text

> Quote removal (see 2.6.7 Quote Removal), if performed, shall always be performed last.

## Context

This defines word expansion step 4: quote removal.

**Required behavior:**
- Quote removal (as defined in 2.6.7) must always be performed last in the expansion pipeline, if it is performed at all.
- Quote removal strips the quoting characters (`\`, `'`, `"`) that were used to protect characters from expansion during earlier steps, leaving only the literal characters they protected.
- After quote removal, the resulting fields are the final arguments/words.

**Conditions:** Quote removal is performed on words that are being prepared for command execution. It is the final step after all other expansions.

See 2.6 "Word Expansions" (step 4) and 2.6.7 "Quote Removal" in `utilities/V3_chap02.html`.
