## Extracted Text

> When unquoted, unescaped, and not inside a bracket expression, the following three characters shall have special meaning in the specification of patterns:: [

## Context

**`[` is a special pattern character.**

The `[` (left-square-bracket) character is one of the three special pattern characters. When unquoted, unescaped, and not inside a bracket expression, it **shall** have special meaning — it introduces a bracket expression. Its specific behavior is in SHALL-19-14-01-013/016.

See 2.14.1 in utilities/V3_chap02.html.
