## Extracted Text

> In a locale definition, the following mandatory keywords shall be recognized:: abday

## Context

The `abday` keyword shall be recognized in an LC_TIME locale definition. It introduces the definition of abbreviated weekday names.

**Shell relevance:** Abbreviated weekday names affect `%a` in `date` and `printf '%(fmt)T'`. See XBD 7.3.5.1.
