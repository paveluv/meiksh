## Extracted Text

> The utility shall process the statefully encoded data as a concatenation of state-independent characters. The presence of redundant locking shifts shall not affect the comparison of two statefully encoded strings.

## Context

**Behavior:** Duplicate of the core requirement from SHALL-06-04-01-003. Utilities must process statefully encoded data as state-independent character sequences, and redundant locking shifts must not affect string comparison.

**Shell relevance:** Same as SHALL-06-04-01-003. The shell must normalize state-dependent encoded strings for comparison. Two byte sequences that decode to the same characters must compare as equal. This is typically handled by converting to wide characters via `mbrtowc()` before comparison.
