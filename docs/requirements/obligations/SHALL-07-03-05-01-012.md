## Extracted Text

> In a locale definition, the following mandatory keywords shall be recognized:: mon

## Context

The `mon` keyword shall be recognized in an LC_TIME locale definition. It introduces the definition of full month names.

**Shell relevance:** Full month names affect `%B` in `date`. See XBD 7.3.5.1.
