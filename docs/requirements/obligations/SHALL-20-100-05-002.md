## Extracted Text

> The following operand shall be supported:: var

## Context

**Behavior required:** The `read` builtin must accept one or more `var` operands. Each `var` is the name of a shell variable to receive a portion of the input.

**Condition:** All invocations of `read`. At least one `var` must be provided.

**Implementation detail:** The `var` operands are positional arguments after options. Each must be a valid shell variable name (per shell grammar: starts with letter or underscore, followed by alphanumerics or underscores). The variables may or may not already exist. The detailed behavior of `var` is in SHALL-20-100-05-003.
