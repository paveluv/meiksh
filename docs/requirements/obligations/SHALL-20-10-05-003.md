## Extracted Text

> The following operand shall be supported:: Specify the job to be resumed as a background job. If no job_id operand is given, the most recently suspended job shall be used. The format of job_id is described in XBD 3.182 Job ID .

## Context

The `job_id` operand specifies which job to resume as a background job. If no `job_id` operand is given, the most recently suspended job (the "current job", `%%` or `%+`) **shall** be used. The format of `job_id` is described in XBD 3.182: it can be `%n` (job number), `%string` (command name prefix), `%?string` (command containing string), `%%`/`%+` (current job), or `%-` (previous job). Reference: bg utility OPERANDS, XBD 3.182.
