## Extracted Text

> The removal of <backslash><newline> for line continuation (see 2.2.1 Escape Character (Backslash)) shall be performed during the search for the trailing delimiter. (As a consequence, the trailing delimiter is not recognized immediately after a <newline> that was removed by line continuation.) It is unspecified whether the line containing the trailing delimiter is itself subject to this line continuation.

## Context

This is a duplicate extraction of the line-continuation rule from SHALL-19-07-04-004. See 2.7.4 Here-Document in utilities/V3_chap02.html and 2.2.1 Escape Character (tag_19_02_01).

**Behavior required:**
- When the delimiter is unquoted, `\<newline>` line continuation SHALL be performed during the search for the trailing delimiter line.
- The trailing delimiter is not recognized immediately after a newline removed by line continuation.
- It is UNSPECIFIED whether the trailing delimiter line itself is subject to line continuation.

See SHALL-19-07-04-004 for full context. This obligation and SHALL-19-07-04-004 cover the same requirement from the same source line.
