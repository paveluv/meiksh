## Context

**Behavior:** The `getopts` utility must accept the operands defined in the OPERANDS section: `optstring`, `name`, and optionally `param...`. This is a framing statement establishing that the listed operands are mandatory to support. The specific semantics of each operand are covered by SHALL-20-53-05-002 through SHALL-20-53-05-008.

**Synopsis:** `getopts optstring name [param...]`
