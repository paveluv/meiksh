## Extracted Text

> The time utility shall invoke the utility named by the utility operand with arguments supplied as the argument operands and write a message to standard error that lists timing statistics for the utility. The message shall include the following information:: The elapsed (real) time between invocation of utility and its termination.

## Context

**Behavior:** The timing message written by `time` shall include the elapsed (real/wall-clock) time between invocation of the utility and its termination.

**Implementation:** Record `clock_gettime(CLOCK_MONOTONIC)` (or equivalent) before and after executing the utility. The difference is the elapsed time.

**Spec reference:** `utilities/time.html` lines 41-42.
