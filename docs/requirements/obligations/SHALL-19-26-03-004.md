## Extracted Text

> The set special built-in shall support XBD 12.2 Utility Syntax Guidelines except that options can be specified with either a leading <hyphen-minus> (meaning enable the option) or <plus-sign> (meaning disable it) unless otherwise specified.

## Context

**Behavior:** The `set` special built-in must support XBD 12.2 Utility Syntax Guidelines, with a special extension: options can be specified with either:
- A leading `-` (hyphen-minus) to **enable** the option.
- A leading `+` (plus-sign) to **disable** the option.

This dual-prefix convention is unique to `set` and must be handled by the option parser.

**Applies to:** All shell modes.
