## Context

**Behavior:** The `kill` built-in must respect the standard internationalization environment variables: LANG, LC_ALL, LC_CTYPE, LC_MESSAGES, and NLSPATH (XSI). These affect locale selection for character interpretation and diagnostic messages. Individual variable details are in SHALL-20-64-08-002 through SHALL-20-64-08-011.
