## Extracted Text

> Otherwise, the token identifier TOKEN shall result.

## Context

This is the fallback/default classification rule: if none of the preceding rules (operator, IO_NUMBER, IO_LOCATION) match, the token shall be classified as `TOKEN`. TOKEN is a generic identifier that will be further refined by the grammar rules in Section 2.10.2 into WORD, NAME, ASSIGNMENT_WORD, or a reserved word, depending on syntactic context.

This ensures every lexical unit produced by the tokenizer receives a classification—there are no unclassified tokens.
