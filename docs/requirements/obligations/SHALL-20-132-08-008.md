## Extracted Text

> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of umask:: LC_MESSAGES

## Context

**Behavior:** LC_MESSAGES shall determine the locale used to affect the format and contents of diagnostic messages written to stderr by the `umask` utility.

**Conditions:** This affects error messages produced by `umask`, such as when an invalid mask operand is provided or an unknown option is used.
