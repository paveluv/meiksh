## Extracted Text

> Specify the job to be run as a foreground job. If no job_id operand is given, the job_id for the job that was most recently suspended, placed in the background, or run as a background job shall be used. The format of job_id is described in XBD 3.182 Job ID.

## Context

This obligation (duplicate extraction of the `job_id` operand description) reiterates that: (1) `fg` brings the specified job to the foreground. (2) Without an explicit `job_id`, the "current job" is used — determined by recency of suspension, backgrounding, or background launch. (3) The `job_id` format follows XBD 3.182. The implementation must maintain a "current job" pointer that tracks the most recently backgrounded/suspended job for this default selection. Reference: POSIX.1-2024 §fg, OPERANDS (tag_20_45_05); XBD 3.182.
