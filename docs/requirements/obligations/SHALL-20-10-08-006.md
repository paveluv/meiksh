> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of bg:: LC_CTYPE

## Context

`LC_CTYPE` **shall** affect the execution of `bg`. It determines the locale for character classification — how byte sequences in arguments are interpreted as characters (single-byte vs. multi-byte). Reference: bg utility ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES.
