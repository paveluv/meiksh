## Extracted Text

> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of getopts:: Determine the locale for the interpretation of sequences of bytes of text data as characters (for example, single-byte as opposed to multi-byte characters in arguments and input files).

## Context

**Behavior:** Describes the effect of `LC_CTYPE`: determines how byte sequences in arguments are interpreted as characters. For `getopts`, this affects how option characters in `optstring` and in command-line arguments are recognized when multi-byte character sets are in use.

**Spec reference:** POSIX.1-2024, `getopts` ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES, line 146 of `utilities/getopts.html`.
