## Extracted Text

> If ECHOK and ICANON are set, the KILL character shall either cause the terminal to erase the line from the display or shall echo the <newline> character after the KILL character.

## Context

**Behavior:** When both ECHOK and ICANON are set in `c_lflag`, the KILL character shall either erase the entire line from the display or echo a newline character after the KILL character.

**Conditions:** Both ECHOK and ICANON must be set. This only applies in canonical mode.

**Relevance to meiksh:** The `stty` builtin must support `echok`/`-echok`. The choice of erasing the line vs. echoing a newline is implementation-defined.

**Spec reference:** POSIX.1-2024, §11.2.5 Local Modes (tag_11_02_05), `basedefs/V1_chap11.html` line 1179.
