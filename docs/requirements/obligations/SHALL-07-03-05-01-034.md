## Extracted Text

> In a locale definition, the following mandatory keywords shall be recognized:: alt_digits

## Context

The `alt_digits` keyword shall be recognized in an LC_TIME locale definition. It introduces the definition of alternative digit symbols.

**Shell relevance:** Alternative digits affect `%O` modified conversions in `date`. See XBD 7.3.5.1.
