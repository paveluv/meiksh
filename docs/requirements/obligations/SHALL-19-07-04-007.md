## Extracted Text

> The delimiter shall be the word itself.

## Context

This is a duplicate extraction of the unquoted-delimiter rule from SHALL-19-07-04-003. See 2.7.4 Here-Document in utilities/V3_chap02.html.

**Behavior required:**
- When no part of the here-document delimiter `word` is quoted, the delimiter shall be the `word` itself, used as-is for matching the terminating line.
- Example: `<<EOF` means the delimiter is the literal string `EOF`.

See SHALL-19-07-04-003 for full context. This obligation and SHALL-19-07-04-003 cover the same requirement from the same source line.
