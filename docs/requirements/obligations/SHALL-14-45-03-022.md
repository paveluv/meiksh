## Extracted Text

> The range SIGRTMIN through SIGRTMAX inclusive shall include at least {RTSIG_MAX} signal numbers. The value of SIGRTMAX shall be less than the value returned by sysconf(_SC_NSIG).

## Context

The realtime signal range must contain at least `RTSIG_MAX` signals (POSIX minimum is 8). `SIGRTMAX` must be less than `sysconf(_SC_NSIG)`. For `meiksh`, this means when iterating over all valid signals (e.g., for `trap -l` or `kill -l`), the shell must account for realtime signals up to `SIGRTMAX` and must not exceed `sysconf(_SC_NSIG) - 1` as the maximum valid signal number. The shell's signal table must be sized or dynamically allocated to cover the full range.
