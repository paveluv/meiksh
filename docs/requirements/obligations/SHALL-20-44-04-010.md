## Extracted Text

> The following options shall be supported:: Reverse the order of the commands listed (with -l) or edited (with neither -l nor -s).

## Context

This is the full description of the `-r` option for `fc`. The reversal applies in two contexts: (1) In list mode (`-l`): commands are listed in reverse order from what `first` and `last` would normally produce. (2) In edit mode (neither `-l` nor `-s`): commands are presented to the editor in reverse order. The equivalence holds: `fc -r 10 20` is the same as `fc 20 10`, and `fc 20 10` is the same as `fc -r 10 20`. The `-r` option does not apply to the `-s` (re-execute) form. Reference: POSIX.1-2024 §fc, OPTIONS (tag_20_44_04).
