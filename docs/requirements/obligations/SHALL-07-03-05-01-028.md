## Extracted Text

> In a locale definition, the following mandatory keywords shall be recognized:: era_d_fmt

## Context

The `era_d_fmt` keyword shall be recognized in an LC_TIME locale definition. It introduces the definition of the date format in alternative era notation.

**Shell relevance:** Used by `%Ex` in `date`. See XBD 7.3.5.1.
