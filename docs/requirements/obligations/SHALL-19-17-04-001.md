## Extracted Text

> This utility shall not recognize the "--" argument in the manner specified by Guideline 10 of XBD 12.2 Utility Syntax Guidelines.

## Context

The colon (`:`) special builtin utility must **not** recognize `--` as an end-of-options marker in the manner specified by Guideline 10 of XBD 12.2 Utility Syntax Guidelines (basedefs/V1_chap12.html#tag_12_02). Guideline 10 says that `--` should be accepted to indicate end of options, but the colon utility is exempt from this.

This means `: -- foo` does not treat `--` as a special separator. All arguments to `:` are simply expanded (for side effects like variable assignment in the expansion) and then discarded. The `--` is just another argument.

See 2.17 colon in utilities/V3_chap02.html#tag_19_17 and basedefs/V1_chap12.html#tag_12_02 for the Utility Syntax Guidelines.
