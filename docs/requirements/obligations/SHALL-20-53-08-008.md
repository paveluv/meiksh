## Extracted Text

> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of getopts:: LC_MESSAGES

## Context

**Behavior:** `LC_MESSAGES` shall affect `getopts` execution by determining the locale used for diagnostic messages written to stderr (when `optstring` does not begin with `:` and an error is detected).

**Spec reference:** POSIX.1-2024, `getopts` ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES, line 148 of `utilities/getopts.html`.
