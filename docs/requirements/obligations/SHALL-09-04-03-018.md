## Extracted Text

> The <dollar-sign> shall be special when used as an anchor.

## Context

This is a duplicate extraction of the rule from SHALL-09-04-03-013. The `$` character is special only as an end-of-string anchor (Section 9.4.9). When not in an anchoring context (e.g., inside a bracket expression, or when escaped with `\$`), it matches the literal `$` character. The ERE engine must recognize `$` as an anchor at the end of an ERE or subexpression and treat it as literal in all other positions.
