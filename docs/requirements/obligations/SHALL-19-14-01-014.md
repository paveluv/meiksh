## Extracted Text

> A <question-mark> is a pattern that shall match any character.

## Context

**Duplicate of SHALL-19-14-01-009: `?` matches any single character.**

Same obligation as SHALL-19-14-01-009. An unquoted, unescaped `?` not inside a bracket expression **shall** match any single character. See SHALL-19-14-01-009 for full details.
