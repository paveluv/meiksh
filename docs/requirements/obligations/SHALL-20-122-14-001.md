## Extracted Text

> If the utility utility is invoked, the exit status of time shall be the exit status of utility; otherwise, the time utility shall exit with one of the following values:

## Context

**Behavior:** The exit status of `time` depends on whether the utility was successfully invoked:
1. **If utility is invoked:** `time`'s exit status shall be the exit status of the invoked utility.
2. **If utility is NOT invoked:** `time` shall exit with one of: 1-125 (error in time itself), 126 (utility found but could not be invoked), or 127 (utility could not be found).

**Spec reference:** `utilities/time.html` lines 201-202.
