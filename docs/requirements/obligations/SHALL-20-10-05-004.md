## Extracted Text

> Specify the job to be resumed as a background job. If no job_id operand is given, the most recently suspended job shall be used. The format of job_id is described in XBD 3.182 Job ID .

## Context

This reiterates the `job_id` operand behavior for `bg`: the operand specifies which job to resume as a background job. When no `job_id` is given, the most recently suspended job **shall** be used (the "current job"). The `job_id` format follows XBD 3.182. The shell must maintain a job table tracking suspended/background jobs and identify the "current" and "previous" jobs for `%%`/`%+` and `%-` references. Reference: bg utility OPERANDS, XBD 3.182.
