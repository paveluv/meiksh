## Extracted Text

> The <circumflex> shall be special when used as an anchor (see 9.3.8 BRE Expression Anchoring ). The <circumflex> shall signify a non-matching list expression when it occurs first in a list, immediately following a <left-square-bracket> (see 9.3.5 RE Bracket Expression).

## Context

**Behavior:** Duplicate of SHALL-09-03-03-003: The circumflex (`^`) shall be special as a BRE anchor and as a non-matching list indicator in bracket expressions.

**Shell relevance:** Same as SHALL-09-03-03-003. In shell bracket expressions, both `^` and `!` serve as negation indicators.
