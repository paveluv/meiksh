## Extracted Text

> The following tokens shall be processed (in addition to those string constants shown in the grammar):: COLLSYMBOL

## Context

The COLLSYMBOL token shall be recognized in the locale definition grammar. It represents a collation symbol.

**Shell relevance:** Collation symbols affect bracket expressions in pattern matching and regular expressions. See XBD 7.4.1.
