## Extracted Text

> POSIX Locale Value: abmon -- "May"

## Context

In the POSIX locale, the `abmon` keyword's fifth value (for the abbreviated month of May) is "May". This is a data point for the POSIX locale definition. The word "May" here is a month abbreviation, not an obligation keyword.

**Shell relevance:** When LC_TIME is the POSIX locale, `date +%b` for month 5 shall produce "May". See XBD 7.3.5.3.
