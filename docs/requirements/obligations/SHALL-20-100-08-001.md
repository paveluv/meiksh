> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of read:

## Context

**Behavior required:** The `read` builtin must honor the listed environment variables: IFS, LANG, LC_ALL, LC_CTYPE, LC_MESSAGES, NLSPATH, and PS2.

**Condition:** Every invocation of the `read` builtin.

**Implementation detail:** This is a preamble obligation. The individual variables are defined in SHALL-20-100-08-002 through SHALL-20-100-08-016. No standalone action needed.

**Spec reference:** Section ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES of `utilities/read.html`, line 147.
