## Context

**Behavior:** The `kill` built-in must accept the operands defined in the OPERANDS section: `pid` (one or more process/process-group identifiers or job IDs) and `exit_status` (used only with `-l`). This is the introductory statement; the specific operand requirements are in SHALL-20-64-05-002 through SHALL-20-64-05-006.
