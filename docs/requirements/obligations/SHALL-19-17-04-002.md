## Extracted Text

> Implementations shall not support any options.

## Context

The colon (`:`) special builtin must **not** support any options. There are no `-x`, `-h`, or any other flags. All arguments are treated as regular operands (expanded for side effects and discarded).

Any argument starting with `-` is not an option — it is just an argument. For example, `: -x` does not enable anything; `-x` is expanded and ignored.

See 2.17 colon in utilities/V3_chap02.html#tag_19_17.
