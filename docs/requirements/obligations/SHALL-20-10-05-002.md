## Extracted Text

> The following operand shall be supported:: job_id

## Context

The `bg` builtin **shall** support the `job_id` operand. The `job_id` identifies the job to resume as a background job. Its format follows XBD 3.182 (Job ID): `%number`, `%string`, `%?string`, `%%`, `%+`, `%-`, etc. Reference: bg utility OPERANDS, XBD 3.182.
