## Extracted Text

> Weights shall be assigned such that the collation sequence has a total ordering of all characters unless an '@' modifier in the locale name indicates that it has a special collation sequence.

## Context

The LC_COLLATE weight assignment must produce a total ordering of all characters (every pair of distinct characters has a defined relative order) unless the locale name includes an `@` modifier indicating a special collation sequence (e.g., `fr_FR@dict`). The shell is affected because a total ordering guarantees that bracket expression ranges like `[a-z]` have deterministic behavior — every character is either in the range or not. When the `@` modifier is present, the collation sequence may have special properties (e.g., dictionary ordering) that alter range membership.

See: `basedefs/V1_chap07.html` §7.3.2.4 Collation Order.
