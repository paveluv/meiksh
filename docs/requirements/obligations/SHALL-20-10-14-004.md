## Extracted Text

> The following exit values shall be returned:: >0

## Context

Exit status >0 **shall** be returned by `bg` when an error occurred. Errors include: job control is disabled, the specified `job_id` does not exist, or the job could not be resumed. Reference: bg utility EXIT STATUS.
