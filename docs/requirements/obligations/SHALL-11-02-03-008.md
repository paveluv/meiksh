## Extracted Text

> Horizontal-tab delay type 1 shall be dependent on the current column position. Type 2 shall be about 0.10 seconds. Type 3 specifies that <tab> characters shall be expanded into <space> characters. If OFILL is set, two fill characters shall be transmitted for any delay.

## Context

**Behavior:** Horizontal-tab delay types (TABDLY mask in `c_oflag`):
- TAB1 (type 1): delay is dependent on the current column position.
- TAB2 (type 2): delay is about 0.10 seconds.
- TAB3 (type 3): tab characters shall be expanded into space characters (tab-to-spaces expansion).
When OFILL is set, two fill characters shall be transmitted for any tab delay.

**Relevance to meiksh:** The `stty` builtin must support setting TAB0, TAB1, TAB2, TAB3 via the TABDLY mask. TAB3 (tab expansion) is particularly relevant as `stty tabs`/`stty -tabs` typically maps to this.

**Spec reference:** POSIX.1-2024, §11.2.3 Output Modes (tag_11_02_03), `basedefs/V1_chap11.html` line 804.
