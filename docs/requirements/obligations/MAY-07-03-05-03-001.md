## Extracted Text

> POSIX Locale Value: mon -- "May"

## Context

In the POSIX locale, the `mon` keyword's fifth value (for the month of May) is "May". This is a data point for the POSIX locale definition of full month names. The word "May" here is a month name, not an obligation keyword.

**Shell relevance:** When LC_TIME is the POSIX locale, `date +%B` for month 5 shall produce "May". See XBD 7.3.5.3.
