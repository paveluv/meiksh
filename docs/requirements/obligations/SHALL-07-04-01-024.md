## Extracted Text

> The following tokens shall be processed (in addition to those string constants shown in the grammar):: EXTENDED_REG_EXP

## Context

The EXTENDED_REG_EXP token shall be recognized in the locale definition grammar. It represents an extended regular expression.

**Shell relevance:** Extended regular expressions are used in locale definition for LC_MESSAGES yesexpr/noexpr. See XBD 7.4.1.
