## Extracted Text

> If the pipeline begins with the reserved word ! and command1 is a subshell command, the application shall ensure that the ( operator at the beginning of command1 is separated from the ! by one or more <blank> characters. The behavior of the reserved word ! immediately followed by the ( operator is unspecified.

## Context

**Behavior:** If a pipeline begins with `!` (logical negation) and the first command is a subshell `(...)`, the application MUST ensure a blank separates `!` from `(`. The behavior of `!(` (without a blank) is UNSPECIFIED.

**Applies to:** Pipelines starting with `!` followed by a subshell command. Example: `! (cmd)` is correct; `!(cmd)` has unspecified behavior.

**Implementation detail:** The parser may choose to reject `!(` as a syntax error, treat it as `! (`, or handle it in any other way. For robustness, meiksh could accept both forms, but this is not required. The key is that the shell is not obligated to handle the no-blank case correctly.

**Spec reference:** Section 2.9.2, lines 2019-2021 in `utilities/V3_chap02.html`.
