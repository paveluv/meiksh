## Extracted Text

> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of read:: LC_CTYPE

## Context

**Behavior required:** The `LC_CTYPE` environment variable must affect the execution of the `read` builtin. It determines how bytes are interpreted as characters.

**Condition:** When `read` performs character-level operations on input.

**Implementation detail:** LC_CTYPE affects:
- How bytes in IFS form characters (single-byte vs multi-byte)
- Which characters in IFS are considered "IFS whitespace"
- Interpretation of sequences of bytes as characters in arguments

This is particularly important for multi-byte locale support where IFS characters may be multi-byte.
