## Extracted Text

> In a locale definition, the following mandatory keywords shall be recognized:: day

## Context

The `day` keyword shall be recognized in an LC_TIME locale definition. It introduces the definition of full weekday names.

**Shell relevance:** Full weekday names affect `%A` in `date` and `printf '%(fmt)T'`. See XBD 7.3.5.1.
