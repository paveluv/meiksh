## Extracted Text

> In a locale definition, the following mandatory keywords shall be recognized:: am_pm

## Context

The `am_pm` keyword shall be recognized in an LC_TIME locale definition. It introduces the definition of ante-meridiem and post-meridiem strings.

**Shell relevance:** AM/PM strings affect `%p` in `date`. In the POSIX locale: "AM";"PM". See XBD 7.3.5.1.
