## Extracted Text

> The following options shall be supported:: -l

## Context

**Behavior:** The `kill` built-in must support the `-l` option (the letter ell). When `-l` is specified, the utility enters list mode rather than signal-sending mode.

**Conditions:** The `-l` option may appear alone (to list all signal names) or with an `exit_status` operand (to map a status to a signal name). It is mutually exclusive with `-s signal_name` and the signal-sending forms.
