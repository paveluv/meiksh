## Extracted Text

> The ']' character; "\]" shall match a ']' character

## Context

**Behavior:** Duplicate/subset of SHALL-09-03-02-001: In BRE context, `\]` shall match a literal `]` character.

**Shell relevance:** Same as SHALL-09-03-02-001. The BRE backslash-escape for `]` does not directly apply to shell patterns, where `]` handling inside bracket expressions follows section 9.3.5 rules (first position special handling).
