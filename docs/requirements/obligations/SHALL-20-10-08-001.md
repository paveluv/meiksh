## Extracted Text

> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of bg:

## Context

The `bg` builtin's execution **shall** be affected by the standard internationalization environment variables: `LANG`, `LC_ALL`, `LC_CTYPE`, `LC_MESSAGES`, and (XSI) `NLSPATH`. These affect character interpretation in arguments and diagnostic message formatting. Reference: bg utility ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES.
