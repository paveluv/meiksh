## Extracted Text

> When unquoted, unescaped, and not inside a bracket expression, the following three characters shall have special meaning in the specification of patterns:: *

## Context

**`*` is a special pattern character.**

The `*` (asterisk) character is one of the three special pattern characters. When unquoted, unescaped, and not inside a bracket expression, it **shall** have special meaning. Its multi-character matching behavior is defined in 2.14.2 Patterns Matching Multiple Characters (see SHALL-19-14-01-011/015 and SHALL-19-14-02-*).

See 2.14.1 in utilities/V3_chap02.html.
