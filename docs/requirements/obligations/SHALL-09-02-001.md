## Extracted Text

> The requirements in this section shall apply to both basic and extended regular expressions.

## Context

**Behavior:** The requirements in section 9.2 shall apply to both Basic Regular Expressions (BRE) and Extended Regular Expressions (ERE).

**Shell relevance:** The shell does not use BRE/ERE directly, but section 9.3.5 (RE Bracket Expression) is shared with shell pattern matching (section 2.14). The general requirements in 9.2 (NUL handling, case-insensitive matching rules, minimum RE length support) apply to bracket expressions used within shell patterns.
