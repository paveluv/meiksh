## Extracted Text

> The following operands shall be supported:: argument

## Context

**Behavior:** The `command` built-in must support `argument` operands. These are strings passed as arguments to the `command_name` being invoked. Zero or more arguments may follow the command_name.

**Applies to:** `command [-p] command_name [argument ...]` invocations.

**Spec reference:** POSIX.1-2024, `utilities/command.html`, section OPERANDS (tag_20_22_05), line 123.
