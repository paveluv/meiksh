## Extracted Text

> The following options shall be supported:: -r

## Context

**Behavior required:** The `read` builtin must support the `-r` option (raw mode).

**Condition:** When `-r` is specified on the `read` command line.

**Implementation detail:** The `-r` option disables backslash escape processing. Its effect is defined in SHALL-20-100-04-006: backslash characters are treated as literal input, not as escape characters. This means no line continuation on `\<newline>` and no escaping of IFS characters.
