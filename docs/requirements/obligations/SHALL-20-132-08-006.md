## Extracted Text

> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of umask:: LC_CTYPE

## Context

**Behavior:** LC_CTYPE shall determine the locale for interpretation of sequences of bytes of text data as characters for the `umask` utility. This affects how multi-byte characters in arguments are interpreted.

**Conditions:** LC_CTYPE is relevant when the mask operand or option arguments contain characters that could be interpreted differently depending on the locale (e.g., single-byte vs. multi-byte encodings).
