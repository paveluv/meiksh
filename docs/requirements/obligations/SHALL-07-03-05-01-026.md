## Extracted Text

> In a locale definition, the following mandatory keywords shall be recognized:: era

## Context

The `era` keyword shall be recognized in an LC_TIME locale definition. It introduces the definition of era description segments for locale-specific year counting.

**Shell relevance:** Era information affects `%EC`, `%Ey`, `%EY` in `date`. See XBD 7.3.5.1.
