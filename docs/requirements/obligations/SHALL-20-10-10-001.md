## Extracted Text

> The output of bg shall consist of a line in the format:

## Context

The output of `bg` **shall** consist of a line in the format: `"[%d] %s\n", job-number, command` — where `job-number` is the job's number in the shell's job table (the number used with `%n`) and `command` is the command string associated with the job. This output is written to stdout when a job is successfully placed in the background. Reference: bg utility STDOUT.
