## Extracted Text

> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of read:: Determine the internal field separators used to delimit fields; see 2.5.3 Shell Variables.

## Context

**Behavior required:** This is the descriptive text for the `IFS` variable's effect on `read`: IFS determines the internal field separators used to delimit fields during the `read` builtin's field splitting.

**Condition:** During field splitting of `read` input.

**Implementation detail:** The value of IFS at the time `read` is invoked determines field splitting behavior. Per Section 2.5.3, IFS defaults to `<space><tab><newline>` when unset. IFS characters are categorized as "IFS whitespace" (space, tab, newline that appear in IFS) and "IFS non-whitespace" (all other IFS characters). This distinction matters for how leading/trailing delimiters and adjacent delimiters are handled.

**Spec reference:** Section ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES of `utilities/read.html`, line 151; Section 2.5.3.
