## Extracted Text

> The concatenation of patterns matching a single character is a valid pattern that shall match the concatenation of the single characters or collating elements matched by each of the concatenated patterns.

## Context

**Duplicate of SHALL-19-14-02-002: concatenation of single-character patterns matches their concatenation.**

Same obligation as SHALL-19-14-02-002. The concatenation of single-character matching patterns **shall** match the concatenation of the characters each sub-pattern matches. See SHALL-19-14-02-002 for full details.
