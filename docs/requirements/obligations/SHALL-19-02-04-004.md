## Extracted Text

> A sequence of characters starting with a <dollar-sign> immediately followed by a single-quote ($') shall preserve the literal value of all characters up to an unescaped terminating single-quote ('), with the exception of certain <backslash>-escape sequences, as follows:: \\ yields a <backslash> character.

## Context

**Behavior:** Inside `$'...'`, the escape sequence `\\` yields a literal backslash character (`\`).

**Applies to:** All shell modes during `$'...'` escape processing.

**Implementation detail:** When `\` is followed by `\`, emit `\` (0x5C) and advance past both characters. Without this escape, every `\` would be interpreted as the start of an escape sequence.

**Spec reference:** Section 2.2.4, lines 175-176 in `utilities/V3_chap02.html`.
