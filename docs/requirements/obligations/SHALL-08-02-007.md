## Extracted Text

> This variable shall determine the locale category for monetary-related numeric formatting information. Additional semantics of this variable, if any, are implementation-defined.

## Context

**Behavior:** The environment variable `LC_MONETARY` shall determine the locale category for monetary-related numeric formatting (currency symbol, decimal separator for currency, grouping of monetary digits, etc.).

**Shell relevance:** The shell itself does not perform monetary formatting, but it must propagate `LC_MONETARY` to child processes. Built-in utilities like `printf` do not use monetary formatting. This obligation is primarily about ensuring the shell does not clobber or fail to inherit `LC_MONETARY` in the environment.

**Additional semantics:** Any further behavior beyond monetary formatting is implementation-defined.
