## Extracted Text

> It is implementation-defined whether realtime signal behavior is supported for other signals.

## Context

The platform may or may not support realtime signal semantics (queuing, ordering, value delivery) for signals outside the `SIGRTMIN`–`SIGRTMAX` range. For `meiksh`, this means the shell cannot assume realtime behavior for standard signals like SIGCHLD or SIGUSR1—only signals in the realtime range are guaranteed to have queuing behavior. The shell should document whether it relies on any realtime behavior for non-realtime signals and should handle the possibility of signal merging (non-queued delivery) for standard signals.
