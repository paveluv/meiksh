> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of bg:: Determine the locale for the interpretation of sequences of bytes of text data as characters (for example, single-byte as opposed to multi-byte characters in arguments).

## Context

`LC_CTYPE` **shall** determine the locale for interpreting byte sequences as characters in `bg` arguments (e.g., single-byte vs. multi-byte characters in `job_id` operands). Reference: bg utility ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES.
