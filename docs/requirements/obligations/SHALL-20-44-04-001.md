## Extracted Text

> The fc utility shall conform to XBD 12.2 Utility Syntax Guidelines.

## Context

The `fc` builtin must parse its options according to the standard Utility Syntax Guidelines (XBD 12.2). This means: options are single characters preceded by `-`, option-arguments follow the option character (with or without a space), `--` terminates option processing, and options can be grouped (e.g., `-ln` is equivalent to `-l -n`). The three synopsis forms are: `fc [-r] [-e editor] [first [last]]`, `fc -l [-nr] [first [last]]`, and `fc -s [old=new] [first]`. Reference: POSIX.1-2024 §fc, OPTIONS (tag_20_44_04).
