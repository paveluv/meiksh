## Extracted Text

> The following operands shall be supported:: One of the strings treated as an argument to command_name.

## Context

**Behavior:** Each `argument` operand is a string that is passed through to the command_name as one of its arguments. The `command` utility does not interpret or modify these arguments — they are forwarded directly.
