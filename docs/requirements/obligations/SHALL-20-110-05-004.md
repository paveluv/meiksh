## Extracted Text

> The following operands shall be supported:: argument

## Context

This is a label/header obligation for the `argument` operand. The `argument` operands are the positional arguments that follow the `command_file` or that follow `command_name` when `-c` is used. Detailed semantics are in SHALL-20-110-05-005 / SHALL-20-110-05-013.
