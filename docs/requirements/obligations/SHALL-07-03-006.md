## Extracted Text

> Operands shall be characters, collating elements, or strings of characters. Strings shall be enclosed in double-quotes. Literal double-quotes within strings shall be preceded by the <escape character>, described below. When a keyword is followed by more than one operand, the operands shall be separated by <semicolon> characters; <blank> characters shall be allowed both before and after a <semicolon>.

## Context

**Behavior:** Locale definition file operand syntax:
- Operands are characters, collating elements, or double-quoted strings.
- Literal double-quotes in strings must be escaped.
- Multiple operands are separated by semicolons, with optional blanks around them.

**Shell relevance:** The shell does not parse locale definition files. This is `localedef` input syntax. No shell implementation action needed.

**Spec reference:** Section 7.3, lines 140-141 in `basedefs/V1_chap07.html`.
