## Extracted Text

> Field splitting (see 2.6.5 Field Splitting) shall be performed on the portions of the fields generated by step 1.

## Context

This defines word expansion step 2: field splitting.

**Required behavior:**
- Field splitting (as defined in 2.6.5) must be performed on the portions of fields generated by step 1 (tilde/parameter/command/arithmetic expansions).
- Only the **results of expansions** are subject to field splitting, not literal text that was part of the original word. Portions of the word that were quoted or that did not result from expansion are not split.
- Field splitting uses the current value of `IFS` to determine delimiters.

**Conditions:** Field splitting is only performed in contexts where it applies (e.g., command arguments). It is NOT performed in:
- Variable assignment values (`var=$foo` — no splitting)
- `case` word
- Here-document bodies
- Words within double-quotes (though individual unquoted expansions within double-quotes may still split in specific circumstances per `$@` rules)

See 2.6 "Word Expansions" (step 2) and 2.6.5 "Field Splitting" in `utilities/V3_chap02.html`.
