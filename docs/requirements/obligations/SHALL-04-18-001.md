## Extracted Text

> Conforming implementations shall define the manner in which each of the scheduling policies may modify the priorities or otherwise affect the ordering of processes or threads at each of the occurrences listed above. Additionally, conforming implementations shall define in what other circumstances and in what manner each scheduling policy may modify the priorities or affect the ordering of processes or threads.

## Context

This is an OS-level requirement about process/thread scheduling policy documentation. It is not directly relevant to the shell's implementation — the shell does not set or manipulate scheduling policies. The shell may observe scheduling effects when running concurrent pipelines or background jobs, but this is transparent. No shell action is required.

See: `basedefs/V1_chap04.html` §4.18 Scheduling Policy.
