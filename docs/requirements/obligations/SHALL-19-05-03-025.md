## Extracted Text

> The following variables shall affect the execution of the shell:: PPID

## Context

This is the heading obligation for the `PPID` shell variable. It introduces `PPID` as one of the variables that shall affect execution. The detailed semantics are in SHALL-19-05-03-026 and the duplicate SHALL-19-05-03-039.

**Required behavior:**
- The shell must recognize `PPID` as a shell variable with special meaning.

See 2.5.3 "Shell Variables" (`PPID`) in `utilities/V3_chap02.html`.
