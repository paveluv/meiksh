## Extracted Text

> The values that the environment variables may be assigned are not restricted except that they are considered to end with a null byte and the total space used to store the environment and the arguments to the process is limited to {ARG_MAX} bytes.

## Context

Environment variable values are unrestricted except they end at a null byte and the total space for environment + arguments is limited to {ARG_MAX} bytes.

**Shell relevance:** The shell must be aware that excessively large environments can cause `exec` to fail with E2BIG. The total of all exported variable strings plus command arguments must not exceed ARG_MAX. See XBD 8.1.
