## Extracted Text

> A rectangular region of columns and lines on a terminal display. A screen may be a portion of a physical display device or may occupy the entire physical area of the display device.

## Context

**Definition only — no shell relevance.** Defines "screen" as a rectangular region of columns and lines on a terminal display. It may be a portion or the entire physical display.

**Shell impact:** None. This is a curses/terminal concept. The shell's interactive line editor uses the terminal but does not manage "screens" in the curses sense. No action required.
