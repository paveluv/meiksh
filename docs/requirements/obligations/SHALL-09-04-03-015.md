## Extracted Text

> The <right-parenthesis> shall be special when matched with a preceding <left-parenthesis>, both not inside a bracket expression.

## Context

This is a duplicate extraction of the rule from SHALL-09-04-03-005. The `)` character is special only when it closes a group opened by a matching `(`, with both parentheses outside any bracket expression. In all other positions—inside bracket expressions, or without a matching `(`—`)` is an ordinary character that matches itself. The ERE engine must track open parentheses to determine whether a `)` closes a group.
