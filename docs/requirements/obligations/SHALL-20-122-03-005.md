## Extracted Text

> The precision of the timing shall be no less than the granularity defined for the size of the clock tick unit on the system, but the results shall be reported in terms of standard time units (for example, 0.02 seconds, 00:00:00.02, 1m33.75s, 365.21 seconds), not numbers of clock ticks.

## Context

**Behavior:** The precision of timing shall be no less than the granularity of the clock tick on the system (i.e., `1.0 / sysconf(_SC_CLK_TCK)` seconds). Results must be reported in standard time units (e.g., seconds with decimal fraction like `0.02`, or formatted as `1m33.75s`, `00:00:00.02`), NOT as raw clock tick counts.

**Constraint:** The output must use human-readable time units, not hardware-level tick counts.
