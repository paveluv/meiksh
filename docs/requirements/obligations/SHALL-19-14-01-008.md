## Extracted Text

> When unquoted, unescaped, and not inside a bracket expression, the following three characters shall have special meaning in the specification of patterns:: ?

## Context

**`?` is a special pattern character.**

The `?` (question-mark) character is one of the three special pattern characters. When unquoted, unescaped, and not inside a bracket expression, it **shall** have special meaning. Its specific matching behavior is defined in SHALL-19-14-01-009/014: it matches any single character.

See 2.14.1 in utilities/V3_chap02.html.
