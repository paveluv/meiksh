## Extracted Text

> As described in 2.9.1 Simple Commands, variable assignments preceding the invocation of a special built-in utility affect the current execution environment; this shall not be the case with a regular built-in or other utility.

## Context

**Duplicate of SHALL-19-15-003: variable assignments before special built-ins persist in current environment.**

Same obligation as SHALL-19-15-003. Variable assignments preceding a special built-in invocation affect the current execution environment permanently. This **shall not** be the case for regular built-ins or external utilities. See SHALL-19-15-003 and 2.9.1 Simple Commands in utilities/V3_chap02.html for full details.
