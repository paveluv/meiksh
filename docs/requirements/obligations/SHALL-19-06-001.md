## Context

This establishes the mandatory ordering of word expansion stages.

**Required behavior:**
- When word expansions are performed on a given word, they must follow a strict order. The steps are enumerated in the obligations that follow (SHALL-19-06-002 through SHALL-19-06-005):
  1. Tilde expansion, parameter expansion, command substitution, and arithmetic expansion (left-to-right, interleaved — see step 1 details).
  2. Field splitting (on portions generated by step 1).
  3. Pathname expansion (unless `set -f` is in effect).
  4. Quote removal (always last, if performed).

**Conditions:** Not all expansions are performed on every word. Context determines which expansions apply (e.g., assignment values don't get field splitting or pathname expansion; here-document bodies get parameter/command/arithmetic expansion but not field splitting or pathname expansion).

**Implementation note:** The expansion pipeline must be implemented as a sequence of passes in this exact order. Reordering or merging steps incorrectly will produce non-conformant behavior.

See 2.6 "Word Expansions" in `utilities/V3_chap02.html`.
