## Extracted Text

> The following environment variables shall affect the execution of getopts:: LC_CTYPE

## Context

**Behavior:** `LC_CTYPE` shall affect `getopts` execution by determining the locale for character interpretation — specifically how sequences of bytes in arguments are interpreted as characters (single-byte vs. multi-byte). This affects option character recognition in `optstring` and arguments.

**Spec reference:** POSIX.1-2024, `getopts` ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES, line 145 of `utilities/getopts.html`.
